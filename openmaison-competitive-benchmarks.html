What every comparison finds

One wedge, one weak flank

Across all four reports the conclusion rhymes: OpenMaison is rarely out-positioned on capability — it's out-proven and out-priced-for-trust. Same edge, same gap, every time.

OpenMaison's recurring edge
  • Genuine AI assistance — a meeting co-pilot, margin-smart quoting and a studio assistant that work alongside the team, well beyond what rivals market.
  • Real business & finance operations — real-time P&L, per-project margins, commissions and variation-order modelling.
  • Regional fit & distribution — Singapore / SEA workflows and the built-in Qanvast audience.
OpenMaison's recurring gap
  • No customer proof yet — it's pre-launch, while every rival shows named studios and reviews.
  • Opaque pricing, high-friction entry — early-access only, against published prices and free trials.
  • A very broad surface to prove — ~11 modules at once, versus focused, established tools.
